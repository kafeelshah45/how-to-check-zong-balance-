Zong Balance Check Guide

Method 1: USSD Code

The simplest and most convenient way to check your Zong balance is by using the USSD code *100#. Dial this code from your Zong SIM, and you will receive an SMS with your current balance information. This method works for both prepaid and postpaid users.

Method 2: Zong App

Zong offers a mobile app for Android and iOS devices that allow you to manage your account, including checking your balance. Download and install the Zong app from the respective app store, sign in with your Zong number, and you will be able to view your balance on the app's home screen.

Method 4: Customer Service

If you are unable to check your balance using any of the methods above, you can contact Zong customer service for assistance. Dial *828# from your Zong SIM, and you will be connected to a customer service representative who can check your balance for you.
